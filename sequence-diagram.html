Steps displayed in the diagram -

  1. Patient requests treatment with mediclaim details
  2. The Hospital then send an authorisation details to the Insurance Company
  3. These details will be then verified and validated by the admin
  4. The admin then sends a claim verification reply
  5. Based on reply there will be 2 possibilities
    • The mediclaim would be approved and patient provided with necessary medication and treatment.
    • The mediclaim is denied and the Hospital informs denial to the patient.
